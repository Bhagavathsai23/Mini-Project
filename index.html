Welcome to the Income Tax Guide

Get insights on taxes and make informed financial decisions.

Why Pay Tax?

Taxes are crucial for funding public services such as healthcare, education, and infrastructure.

What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a tax imposed by the government on the income earned by individuals and businesses.

Who Needs to Pay Tax?

Individuals and businesses that earn income above certain thresholds are required to pay income tax.

Tips for Tax Benefits

  • Invest in tax-saving instruments such as retirement funds and tax-free bonds.
  • Claim deductions for expenses like education, healthcare, and home loan interest.
