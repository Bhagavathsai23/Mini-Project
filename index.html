Why Pay Tax?

Taxes are crucial for funding public services such as healthcare, education, and infrastructure. They also help reduce economic inequality and support governmental functions.

What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a tax imposed by the government on the income earned by individuals and businesses. It is a key revenue source used to finance various public services and programs.

Who Needs to Pay Tax?

Individuals and businesses that earn income above certain thresholds are required to pay income tax. This requirement helps fund public services and government operations.

What Income is Taxable?

Taxable income includes wages, salaries, bonuses, rental income, and investment income. Specific exemptions and deductions might apply based on your jurisdiction.

Tips for Tax Benefits

  • Invest in tax-saving instruments such as retirement funds and tax-free bonds.
  • Claim deductions for expenses like education, healthcare, and home loan interest.
  • Keep detailed records of all deductible expenses to maximize your tax savings.
