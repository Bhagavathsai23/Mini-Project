Indian Tax Slabs and Rates

For the financial year 2023-24, income tax rates are divided into several slabs. Learn more about the applicable rates here.

  • 5% tax for income up to ₹2,50,000
  • 10% tax for income between ₹2,50,001 and ₹5,00,000
  • 15% tax for income between ₹5,00,001 and ₹7,50,000
  • 20% tax for income between ₹7,50,001 and ₹10,00,000
  • 30% tax for income over ₹10,00,000
